## Notes On Reused Evidence

The v0.7 walkthrough reuses verified v0.5 and v0.6 screenshots where the screen already shows the required narrative state. This keeps the narrative package grounded in already verified evidence instead of introducing product changes during a documentation phase.

Two screenshots are marked as documented replacements:

- `05-merchant-status-sold-out.png` reuses the merchant status screen as a placeholder for the sold-out action moment.
- `07-organizer-exception-approved-v2.png` reuses the exception center screen as a placeholder for the post-approval v2 explanation.

Before final judging or final slide production, these two states should be regenerated with a dedicated v0.7 Playwright capture if the reused screenshots are not specific enough.

## Manual Inspection Checklist

- No screenshot is blank.
- Public and tourist screenshots do not expose internal backend terms.
- Mobile screenshots do not hide the main evidence behind fixed navigation.
- Each screenshot supports one explicit claim.
