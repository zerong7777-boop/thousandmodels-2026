# Initial Idea Matrix

## Scoring Lens

Use the official rubric as the first-pass filter:

- Task completion: can the task reach a visible, verifiable outcome?
- Agent capability: does the system plan, call tools, execute steps, recover, and explain?
- Application innovation: is the Macau scenario specific rather than generic?
- Practice article: can we write a clear method/evidence story?
- Live presentation: can it be demonstrated reliably under time pressure?

## Candidate A: Macau Personal Travel Agent

Concept: a multilingual Macau itinerary Agent that builds personalized half-day or full-day plans under time, budget, mobility, weather, crowd, food preference, and cultural-interest constraints.

Potential Agent abilities:

- Ask clarifying questions.
- Plan routes and time blocks.
- Call map/search/weather/event tools when available.
- Explain tradeoffs and fallback options.
- Generate visitor-facing Chinese/English/Portuguese summaries.

Strengths:

- Strong fit to Macau tourism/culture.
- Easy to demo.
- Clear task-completion loop.
- Good space for multi-step Agent behavior.

Risks:

- Must avoid becoming a generic trip planner.
- Needs Macau-specific data and examples.

Initial recommendation: primary candidate.

## Candidate B: Old District Revitalization Merchant Assistant

Concept: an Agent for old-district small merchants that converts shop information into visitor-facing promotion plans, bilingual descriptions, short routes, event ideas, and social media copy.

Potential Agent abilities:

- Interview merchant profile.
- Generate promotion plan.
- Connect shop to nearby cultural routes.
- Produce alternative content styles.
- Check consistency and safety of claims.

Strengths:

- Strong old-district revitalization relevance.
- Good practice article story.
- Useful for local SMEs.

Risks:

- Task completion may look like content generation unless tool use and workflow are designed carefully.
- Needs representative merchant examples.

Initial recommendation: backup candidate.

## Candidate C: Elder-Friendly Community Service Agent

Concept: an Agent that helps older residents understand community services, plan accessible trips, simplify forms, and notify family or volunteers when needed.

Potential Agent abilities:

- Simplify official information.
- Ask safety-sensitive clarification questions.
- Recommend service paths.
- Produce caregiver summary.
- Escalate uncertain/high-risk requests.

Strengths:

- Strong social value.
- Good elder-friendly services fit.

Risks:

- Health and safety boundaries need careful handling.
- Demo must avoid implying medical or emergency authority.

Initial recommendation: keep as social-impact candidate, but do not choose until risk boundaries are clear.
